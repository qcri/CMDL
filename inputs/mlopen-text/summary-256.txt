Undercover San Francisco narcotics cops Sean Kane and Dave Pierce head into a dark alley to meet up with an informant by the name of Tony Montoya who promises to break their big investigation wide open by providing the name of the oriental drug ringleader. Minutes later, Pierce is dead after having been shot, hit by a car, and burned.
